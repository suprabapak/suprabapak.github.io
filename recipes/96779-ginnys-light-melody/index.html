Ginny S Light Melody

Nutrition : 260 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 410 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 1 roll Hillshire Farm light Polish kielbasa, sliced quarter size
  • 2 c. 2% milk
  • 1 Tbsp. plain flour
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 tsp. pepper
  • 8 oz. shredded cheese
  • 1 large onion, sliced half-dollar size
  • 4 medium potatoes, sliced half-dollar size
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1 c. celery, diced
  • 1 c. bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can English peas or kidney beans
  • 2 c. cabbage, sliced
  • 5 slices light cheese optional

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350u00b0.
  • In large baking dish, add sausage and milk.
  • Bake for 20 minutes.
  • Add shredded cheese, flour, salt and pepper.
  • Stir well.
  • Bake 15 to 20 minutes.
  • Parboil cabbage, onion, potatoes, carrots, celery and bell pepper for about 20 minutes.
  • Add parboiled vegetables to the sausage mixture.
  • Add some vegetable broth if you have room in baking dish.
  • Add English peas or kidney beans.
  • Bake for 30 minutes or until vegetables are done.
  • Cut light cheese slices in half and place on top of casserole for the last 5 minutes of baking time.
