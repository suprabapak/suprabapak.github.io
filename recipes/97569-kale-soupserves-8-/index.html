Kale SoupServes 8

Nutrition : 300 calories,
5 stars - based on 339 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 2 heads kale or collard greens
  • 1/2 head cabbage
  • 2 cans white or red cannellini beans
  • 2 or 3 medium size potatoes
  • 1 or 2 carrots
  • 1 sweet potato
  • pork or chicken optional
  • linguica
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  • Chop up kale or greens, discarding the stalks.
  • Chop cabbage; cut potatoes into cubes.
  • Peel carrots and sweet potatoes.
  • Set all the ingredients aside.
  • You need a large pot filled with water. Add your meat and beans and simmer for 1 to 2 hours or until tender.
  • Add sweet potatoes; cook for awhile.
  • In a large bowl add water to the kale or greens and squeeze out the greens water will be very green.
  • Discard water and add kale to pot with all other ingredients.
  • Make sure you still have enough water and cook until potatoes are tender, stirring frequently.
  • Depending on how much meat you have, this soup could take 3 to 4 hours to cook.
  • Cook on medium heat.
  • Enjoy!
