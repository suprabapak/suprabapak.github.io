Crescent Roll Casserole

Nutrition : 202 calories,
5 stars - based on 70 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 1 can crescent rolls 8 in pkg.
  • 2 chicken breasts or equivalent
  • 4 oz. grated Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 c. milk
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup

Directions

  • Boil chicken and dice into small pieces.
  • Unfold one crescent roll.
  • Put a layer of chicken on roll and then a layer of cheese. Place second crescent on top of first and pinch together. Continue this with other crescents.
  • You will end up with 4 of these.
  • Lay these 4 filled crescents on a large baking dish. Next, heat cream of chicken soup, 1/2 cup milk and remaining cheese until cheese melts.
  • Pour this mixture over rolls.
  • Bake 30 minutes at 350u00b0.
  • Serves 4.
