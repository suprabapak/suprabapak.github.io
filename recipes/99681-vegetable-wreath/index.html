Vegetable Wreath

Nutrition : 169 calories,
5 stars - based on 243 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 3 to 4 bunches parsley
  • 1 bunch broccoli
  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 8 radishes
  • 1 bunch green onions, trimmed

Directions

  • Cover 10 to 12-inch Styrofoam ring completely with foil. Attach parsley to floral picks.
  • Insert into ring, covering top and sides completely.
  • Cut broccoli, cauliflower and green pepper into bite-sized pieces.
  • Make curls of carrots and celery.
  • Cut radishes into roses.
  • Insert wooden toothpick into each vegetable.
  • Stick vegetables into ring.
  • Place ring on serving tray.
  • Place candle or bowl of dip in center of ring.
  • Yields 20 servings.
