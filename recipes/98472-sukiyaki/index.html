Sukiyaki

Nutrition : 172 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 68 reviews
Yield : 4

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp. salad oil
  • 2 lb. round steak, cut in strips
  • 1/2 c. bouillon
  • 3/4 c. soy sauce
  • 1/4 c. sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. sherry
  • 3 onions, sliced thin
  • 1 c. sliced celery
  • 1 can sliced bamboo shoots
  • 1/2 lb. mushrooms, sliced thin
  • 1 can water chestnuts
  • 10 oz. box frozen cooked spinach
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 1 pkg. vermicelli, cooked

Directions

  • Brown meat in hot oil.
  • Combine bouillon, soy sauce, sugar and sherry in bowl.
  • Add 1/2 mixture to meat.
  • Push meat to one side of frying pan.
  • Add onions and celery and cook over low heat 3 minutes.
  • Add remaining bouillon mixture, bamboo shoots, mushrooms, water chestnuts and cooked spinach.
  • Cook for three minutes.
  • Add green onions.
  • Cook 1 hour over low heat.
  • Serve over the cooked vermicelli.
