Johnny S Salsa Cruda

Nutrition : 241 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 225 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. tomatoes, peeled, seeded, and coarsely chopped
  • 1 sm. onion, chopped fine
  • 2 to 3 garlic cloves, put through press
  • 1 chili pepper, seeded and minced
  • 1/4 c. cilantro, chopped
  • 1 Tbsp. lime juice
  • Several twists black pepper

Directions

  • Mix all ingredients together.
  • Best is used fresh but will keep up to 24 hours.
  • Makes 3 1/2 cups.
  • Can also be put into canning jars and sealed.
