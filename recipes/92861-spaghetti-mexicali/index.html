Spaghetti Mexicali

Nutrition : 247 calories,
4 stars - based on 321 reviews
Yield : 3

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. spaghetti
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 medium single onion
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic or equal garlic flakes
  • 1 can cream-style corn
  • 1 can tomatoes small can
  • black or green olives to taste
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 Tbsp. Worcestershire sauce

Directions

  • Cook and drain spaghetti. Saute onion and green pepper. Add beef. Cook until just brown. Add all other ingredients. Blend well. Bake in covered casserole dish for 1 hour at 350u00b0.
