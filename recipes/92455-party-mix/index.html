Party Mix

Nutrition : 299 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 13 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 1/4 c. 1/2 stick butter, melted
  • 1 1/4 tsp. Lawrys seasoned salt
  • 4 1/2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • 8 c. Chex cereal Corn, Rice and/or Wheat
  • 1 c. mixed nuts or peanuts
  • 1 c. butter braids

Directions

  • In a small bowl, add seasoned salt and Worcestershire sauce to melted butter.
  • Mix well.
  • Pour cereal, nuts and pretzels into large Glad-lock zipper bag.
  • Pour margarine mixture over cereal mixture inside zipper bag.
  • Seal top of bag securely. Shake bag until all pieces are evenly coated.
  • Pour contents of bag into large bowl.
  • Microwave on High for 5 to 6 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes. Spread on absorbent paper and cool. Store in airtight container.
  • Makes 9 cups.
