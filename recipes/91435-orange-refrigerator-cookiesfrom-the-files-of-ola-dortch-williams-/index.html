Orange Refrigerator CookiesFrom The Files Of Ola Dortch Williams

Nutrition : 271 calories,
5 stars - based on 145 reviews
Yield : 8

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 c. sifted flour
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/4 tsp. soda
  • 1/4 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 c. shortening
  • 1/2 c. butter
  • 1/2 c. brown sugar, firmly packed
  • 1/2 c. granulated sugar
  • 1 egg unbeaten
  • 2 Tbsp. orange juice
  • 1 Tbsp. grated orange rind
  • 1/2 Tbsp. lemon extract
  • 1 c. finely chopped pecans

Directions

  • Sift together flour, salt, soda and baking powder.
  • In large bowl, combine shortening, butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar and 1 unbeaten egg.
  • Beat on medium-high speed for 1 minute. Gradually add, alternately, sifted flour mixture and orange juice, orange rind and lemon extract.
  • Add 1 cup finely chopped pecans. Mix all and shape into a long roll.
  • Wrap in plastic wrap and chill in refrigerator several hours or overnight.
  • Slice thin and put on a greased cookie sheet.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 for 12 minutes.
