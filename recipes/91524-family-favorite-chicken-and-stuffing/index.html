Family Favorite Chicken And Stuffing

Nutrition : 289 calories,
4.7 stars - based on 73 reviews
Yield : 9

Ingredients

  • 1 6 oz. pkg. instant chicken stuffing mix
  • 1/2 c. chopped carrots
  • 1/2 c. celery
  • 1/2 c. chopped onion
  • 1 c. cream of mushroom soup
  • 1/2 c. milk
  • 1 tsp. parsley
  • 6 skinless chicken breasts
  • paprika

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 400u00b0.
  • Prepare stuffing mix according to package directions, but add vegetables with seasoning packet. Combine soup, milk, and parsley; set aside.
  • In a 2 to 3-quart baking dish, arrange stuffing across center of dish.
  • Spoon thin layer of soup mixture in dish on each side.
  • Arrange chicken over soup mixture.
  • Sprinkle with paprika.
  • Pour remaining soup mixture over chicken.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 15 minutes.
  • Uncover and bake 10 minutes more or until chicken is fork-tender.
