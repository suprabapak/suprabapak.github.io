Breaded Pork Chops - Italian Style

Nutrition : 241 calories,
4.5 stars - based on 160 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 4 to 8 pork loin center cut chops, thin cut
  • 3 eggs
  • pinch of rosemary
  • 1 clove garlic, minced fine
  • pinch of parsley flakes
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • bread crumbs
  • olive oil

Directions

  • Trim pork chops; remove marrow and fat.
  • Wash chops and pat dry.
  • In bowl, beat eggs and add spices.
  • Add pork chops to egg mixture and mix well.
  • Place bowl in freezer for 3 minutes so egg coagulates on chops and bread crumbs stick better.
  • Preheat oven to 375u00b0 with grill in center.
  • Remove chops from freezer; cover both sides with bread crumbs.
  • Place in shallow baking pan sprayed with Pam and lightly spread with olive oil.
  • Cook each side 20 minutes.
