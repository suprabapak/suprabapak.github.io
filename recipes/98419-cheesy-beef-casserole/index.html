Cheesy Beef Casserole

Nutrition : 257 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 218 reviews
Yield : 1

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 to 2 lb. ground beef
  • chopped onion to taste
  • chopped green pepper to taste
  • 1 can 16 oz. green beans
  • 1 can 8 oz. tomato sauce
  • 1 pkg. 8 oz. shredded Mozzarella or Cheddar
  • 2 cans 10 count refrigerated biscuits

Directions

  • In a large skillet, brown ground beef with onions and green pepper.
  • Drain and discard fat.
  • Add green beans and tomato sauce; cook until warm.
  • In a greased 10-inch glass pie plate, pat enough biscuits together to form a bottom crust.
  • Bake in 350u00b0 oven until slightly brown.
  • Remove from oven and spoon ground beef mixture over crust.
  • Arrange shredded cheese over beef mixture.
  • Cut remaining biscuits in halves or quarters and roll in small balls. Arrange on top of cheese.
  • Return to 350u00b0 oven and bake until biscuits are golden brown, 10 to 15 minutes.
  • Makes 6 to 8 servings.
