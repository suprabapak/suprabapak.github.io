Death By Chocolate

Nutrition : 155 calories,
4.6 stars - based on 430 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 1 19.8 oz. brownie mix
  • 3 3.5 oz. each pkg. instant chocolate mousse
  • 8 chocolate covered toffee candy bars 1.4 oz. each, such as Heath or Skor
  • 1 12 oz. container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions

  • Preheat oven and prepare and bake brownies according to package directions; let cool.
  • Prepare chocolate mousse according to package directions.
  • Break candy bars into small pieces.
  • Break brownies into small pieces and place half of them in the bottom of a large glass bowl or trifle dish.
  • Cover with half the mousse, half the candy and half the whipped topping.
  • Repeat layers with remaining ingredients, sprinkling second layer of whipped topping with crushed candy.
  • You may use pecans instead.
