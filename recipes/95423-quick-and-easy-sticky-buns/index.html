Quick And Easy Sticky Buns

Nutrition : 293 calories,
4.1 stars - based on 130 reviews
Yield : 7

Ingredients

  • 2 loaves frozen bread, thawed
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 c. brown sugar
  • 1 small pkg. vanilla pudding, made to serve
  • 2 Tbsp. milk
  • 2 Tbsp. water
  • 1 tsp. cinnamon
  • nuts chopped
  • raisins, optional

Directions

  • Grease 13 x 9 pan.
  • Place nuts and raisins in bottom.
  • Cut each loaf in 8 pieces.
  • In a saucepan, heat the remaining ingredients.
  • Place bread dough on the nuts and raisins.
  • Pour hot mixture over dough SLOWLY.
  • Let rise until doubled. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
