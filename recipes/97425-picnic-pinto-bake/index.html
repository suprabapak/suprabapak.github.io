Picnic Pinto Bake

Nutrition : 169 calories,
4 stars - based on 264 reviews
Yield : 10

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1 16 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 1 sweet onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 2 1/2 c. cooked pinto beans
  • 1 box Jiffy cornbread mix

Directions

  • Brown beef and drain well.
  • Add all ingredients except cornbread mix.
  • Pour in large dish.
  • Mix cornbread as directed on package.
  • Pour over mixture.
  • Bake at 350u00b0 until top is brown.
