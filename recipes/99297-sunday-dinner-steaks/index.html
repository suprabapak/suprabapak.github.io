Sunday Dinner Steaks

Nutrition : 291 calories,
4 stars - based on 295 reviews
Yield : 6

Ingredients

  • 6 to 9 cubed beefsteaks
  • 1 can mushrooms with liquid
  • 1 medium oinon, sliced thin
  • 1 envelope dry onion soup mix
  • 1 can beef gravy or lean mushroom gravy
  • 1 gravy can water

Directions

  • Dredge steak in flour and pepper.
  • Brown lightly on both sides in oil.
  • Place in greased 9 x 13-inch pan.
  • Sprinkle dry soup mix over top.
  • Cover with mushrooms and liquid.
  • Add a layer of sliced onions.
  • Pour can of gravy that has been mixed with water over the top.
  • Do not stir.
