Hearty Tuna CasseroleMicrowave

Nutrition : 190 calories,
3.9 stars - based on 419 reviews
Yield : 5

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chunk tuna
  • 3 c. uncooked egg noodles
  • 1/2 c. chopped celery
  • 1/3 c. sliced green onions
  • 1/2 c. sour cream
  • 2 tsp. mustard
  • 1/2 c. mayonnaise
  • 1/2 tsp. dried thyme leaves
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 small zucchini, scrubbed and sliced
  • 1 c. shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped

Directions

  • Drain and flake tuna.
  • Set aside.
  • Cook noodles according to package directions.
  • Drain and rinse in hot water.
  • Combine noodles with tuna, celery and green onion.
  • Blend in the sour cream, mustard, mayonnaise, thyme and salt.
  • Spoon half the mixture into a buttered 2-quart casserole.
  • Top with half the zucchini.
  • Repeat layers.
  • Cover.
  • Microwave at High for 6 to 8 minutes.
  • Top with cheese and tomato.
  • Microwave at High for 2 minutes; let stand for 3 minutes before serving.
